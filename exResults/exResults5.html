Simple Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. Quick and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 150g guanciale (or pancetta), diced
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
  2. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  3. While pasta cooks, crisp the guanciale in a bowl or pan.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Combine pasta with guanciale. Add egg mixture and a bit of pasta water. Toss quickly.
  6. Serve immediately with more cheese and pepper.
